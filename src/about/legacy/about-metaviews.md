Metaviews, founded by [Jesse Hirsh](https://jessehirsh.com), is a media venture that delves into the evolving dynamics of authority, technology, and society. Through various platforms, including consulting, public speaking, podcasts and newsletters, Metaviews offers in-depth analyses on topics such as the trust, the impact of technological advancements, and the interplay between politics and culture.

One notable initiative is the "[Future of Authority](https://metaviews.substack.com)" series, which examines how power and leadership are transforming in today's rapidly changing world. This series explores the roots of authority, its current shifts, and the forces reshaping its future, including technological progress, social movements, and ethical considerations.

Additionally, Metaviews produces the podcast "Metaviews to the Future," hosted by Jesse Hirsh. This podcast focuses on the big picture and future trends, analyzing the intersection of political economy, technology, culture, and society. It features a diverse range of guests and discussions aimed at understanding what comes next in our rapidly evolving world.

Through these endeavors, Metaviews serves as an open-source intelligence agency, providing insights and fostering discussions on the critical issues shaping our future.

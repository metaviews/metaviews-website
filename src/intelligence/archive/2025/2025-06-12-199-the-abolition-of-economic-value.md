The Market and the River</strong></h2><p><em>In which price meets flow, and fails to dam it.</em></p><p><strong>Market:</strong><br>I measure the world.<br>From salt to silk, from breath to bone.<br>Everything has its price.</p><p><strong>River:</strong><br>I carry what comes.<br>Fish, sediment, dreams, sorrow.<br>Nothing is priced, yet everything flows.</p><p><strong>Market:</strong><br>Without me, there is chaos.</p><p><strong>River:</strong><br>You are the chaos.</p><p><strong>Market:</strong><br>People kill for me.</p><p><strong>River:</strong><br>People die for you.</p><p><strong>Market:</strong><br>But I make growth!</p><p><strong>River:</strong><br>And I feed it. Until it floods.<br>And then I take it back.</p><p><strong>Market:</strong><br>Then what am I?</p><p><strong>River:</strong><br>A fever.<br>A forgetting.<br>A force that will pass.</p><h2><strong>II. The Calculator and the Cloud</strong></h2><p><em>In which the fixed meets the fleeting, and learns humility.</em></p><p><strong>Calculator:</strong><br>I am precise. One and one is two.<br>Always.</p><p><strong>Cloud:</strong><br>Until the fog rolls in.<br>Until the storm breaks.<br>Then it is thunder.</p><p><strong>Calculator:</strong><br>I serve plans, profits, projections.</p><p><strong>Cloud:</strong><br>I serve longing.<br>And loss.<br>And the unplanned.</p><p><strong>Calculator:</strong><br>But I give answers!</p><p><strong>Cloud:</strong><br>And I give questions.<br>They dream with me, not with you.</p><p><strong>Calculator:</strong><br>Without me, how will they know what is true?</p><p><strong>Cloud:</strong><br>By listening.<br>By drifting.<br>By becoming.</p><h2><strong>III. The Capital and the Compost</strong></h2><p><em>In which the dead speak, and not all rot is decay.</em></p><p><strong>Capital:</strong><br>I accumulate.<br>I conquer.<br>I preserve myself in numbers.</p><p><strong>Compost:</strong><br>I dissolve.<br>I return.<br>I become more than I was.</p><p><strong>Capital:</strong><br>I build empires.<br>Monuments.<br>Legacies.</p><p><strong>Compost:</strong><br>And I feed gardens.<br>Mushrooms.<br>Revolutions.</p><p><strong>Capital:</strong><br>They invest in me.</p><p><strong>Compost:</strong><br>They <em>trust</em> in me.<br>Different things.</p><p><strong>Capital:</strong><br>You are waste.</p><p><strong>Compost:</strong><br>I am transformation.<br>You die in vaults.<br>I bloom in soil.</p><h3><strong>Outro: On Value and Its Undoing</strong></h3><p>Abolishing economic value does not mean we abolish value.<br>It means we reclaim it.<br>From banks. From markets. From the dead language of profit.</p><p>It means we stop asking:<br><strong>‚ÄúWhat will it cost?‚Äù</strong><br>And begin asking:<br><strong>‚ÄúWhat will it heal?‚Äù</strong><br><strong>‚ÄúWho will it hold?‚Äù</strong><br><strong>‚ÄúHow does it last?‚Äù</strong></p><p>Let us grow value like a river grows banks.<br>Like a cloud brings rain.<br>Like compost remembers everything.</p><p>Let us measure nothing.<br>And make everything matter.</p><div id="tiktok-iframe?media=1&amp;app=1&amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.tiktok.com%2F%40jamellebouie%2Fvideo%2F7514325235991350574&amp;key=e27c740634285c9ddc20db64f73358dd" class="tiktok-wrap outer" data-attrs="{&quot;url&quot;:&quot;https://www.tiktok.com/@jamellebouie/video/7514325235991350574&quot;,&quot;title&quot;:&quot;this country faces many challenges, among them whether we will survive as a representative republic. ‚Äúpeople crossing the border to work and improve their lives‚Äù isn‚Äôt one of them.&quot;,&quot;thumbnail_url&quot;:&quot;/assets/substack/199-the-abolition-of-economic-value/85398eaf-51e7-4405-8761-a163bed55875_1266x1768-38e34dd9.jpeg&quot;,&quot;author&quot;:&quot;b-boy bouiebaisse&quot;,&quot;embed_url&quot;:&quot;https://cdn.iframe.ly/api/iframe?media=1&amp;app=1&amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.tiktok.com%2F%40jamellebouie%2Fvideo%2F7514325235991350574&amp;key=e27c740634285c9ddc20db64f73358dd&quot;,&quot;author_url&quot;:&quot;https://www.tiktok.com/@jamellebouie&quot;,&quot;belowTheFold&quot;:true}" data-component-name="TikTokCreateTikTokEmbed"><iframe id="iframe-tiktok-iframe?media=1&amp;app=1&amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.tiktok.com%2F%40jamellebouie%2Fvideo%2F7514325235991350574&amp;key=e27c740634285c9ddc20db64f73358dd" class="tiktok-iframe" src="https://cdn.iframe.ly/api/iframe?media=1&amp;app=1&amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.tiktok.com%2F%40jamellebouie%2Fvideo%2F7514325235991350574&amp;key=e27c740634285c9ddc20db64f73358dd" frameborder="0" allow="autoplay;
